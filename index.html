What we do

We at AnuPradhana Seva we're committed to reducing food waste and promoting sustainability through collaborative efforts and innovative solutions. We partner with restaurants, function halls, NGOs, Hostels and other organizations to rescue surplus food and redistribute it to those in need. Through these partnerships, we ensure that excess food is put to good use, reducing waste and alleviating hunger in our communities.
But our impact doesn't stop there. We also implement food recycling initiatives to efficiently utilize any leftover food that cannot be donated. By converting this surplus food into valuable resources, we generate revenue that further supports our mission to combat food waste and create a more sustainable future for all.
